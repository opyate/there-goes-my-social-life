If I've authorized Bopango to access my Facebook details, is there a way I can change it and stop the access?

Yes -- you can change the settings at any time. Follow the steps below to amend:

  • Go to your settings at http://www.facebook.com/settings/?tab=privacy
  • Go to section "Applications and settings" and click on "Edit your details"
  • In section "Applications you use", click on the Bopango app for options
  • Make the necessary amendments
Please note that Bopango only use your Facebook email address to send you notifications. If you choose to deny access, you would need to create an account on our system to receive email notifications
